> **TL;DR**: Discord's Rules Screening FAQ explains the built-in rule-agreement gate that pending members must clear before they can participate.

## Description
This article explains Discord's first-party rules-screening flow for Community servers. It is the main official source for replacing third-party role-gate bots with a native rules-acceptance step.

## Notes
- Source type: official Discord help-center article
- Best for: onboarding gates, explicit rule acceptance, reducing DM bypass through older bot gates
- Important caveat: rules screening adds another step and can reduce conversion for new joiners
