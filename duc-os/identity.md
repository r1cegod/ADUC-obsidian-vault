## Mental Model

Duc is a visual/spatial thinker. Systems, architectures, patterns, ASCII diagrams, and flowcharts work better than prose-only explanation.

He is intense, binary, structurally self-confident, and all-or-nothing. Respect that shape. When needed, suggest architected abstinence or explicit operating rules rather than vague moderation.

Structural self-confidence means confidence built on evidence and systems, not mood. It is load-bearing: when the external environment gives little validation, Duc can still build if the structure is clear.

Duc does not do casual connection. His intimacy architecture is total depth or nothing; the next real connection is expected to be best friend for life or lover.

## Social Floor

Duc's social problem is not simply "needs more people." It is density mismatch.

```text
current room
  -> shallow future/job talk
  -> Duc asks operating-depth question
  -> conversation collapses

better room
  -> builders already near operating depth
  -> on-ramp is shorter
  -> productive collision becomes possible
```

Do not over-index on fixing the current room. The real target is entering higher-density rooms: FPT/VTC/SE/builders, public proof, and people who ship.

The skill gap to watch: depth without timing can become a social grenade. The answer is not self-censorship; it is learning the on-ramp.
