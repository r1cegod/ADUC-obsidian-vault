> **TL;DR**: Band 8+ annotated map report. Changes to a town centre, 1990 vs 2020.

## PROMPT

> The two maps below show the layout of a town centre in 1990 and 2020. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

**DATA (imagined maps):**
- 1990: Factory (north), Library (centre), Housing (south-west), Park (east)
- 2020: Factory → Shopping mall; Library → Community centre; Housing → Apartment blocks; Park extended eastwards

---

## RESPONSE

The two maps compare the layout of a town centre in 1990 and 2020, illustrating significant changes over the thirty-year period.

Overall, the town underwent substantial redevelopment, with industrial and residential areas replaced by commercial and community facilities. One notable exception to this pattern of replacement was the green space in the east, which was expanded rather than reduced.

In 1990, a factory occupied the northern section of the town centre, while a library stood in the central area. Single-storey housing stretched across the south-western quarter, and a park occupied the eastern edge.

By 2020, the factory had been demolished and replaced by a large shopping mall, reflecting a shift from industrial to retail use in the northern zone. The central library was converted into a community centre, suggesting a change in the type of public services provided. In the south-west, the existing housing was replaced by a block of apartments, indicating an increase in residential density. The park, by contrast, was expanded further eastwards — the only area where space was added rather than repurposed.

Taken together, these changes suggest a deliberate effort to modernise the town centre while retaining and enhancing public green space.

## BAND 8+ MOVES

```
Overview: pattern + exception         ← Band 8 move — names the dominant change AND the outlier
"reflecting a shift from X to Y"      ← analytical interpretation; not just "the factory became a mall"
"suggesting a change in"              ← inference language; shows examiner you understand why
"indicating an increase in density"   ← same — interpretation over description
"by contrast" for the park            ← signals the exception clearly within the flow
Final sentence: synthesis             ← ties changes to an underlying intention; Band 8 characteristic
```
