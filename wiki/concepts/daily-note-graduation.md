> **TL;DR**: Daily note graduation is the practice of promoting stable ideas, decisions, and patterns out of raw daily notes into durable concept, synthesis, or project pages.

## Definition
Daily notes are the raw stream. Graduation is the moment when something from that stream proves durable enough to deserve its own page, link surface, or operational home.

## Key Ideas
- Daily notes are good for chronology, not long-term discoverability.
- Graduation should promote patterns, workflows, decisions, or recurring questions that matter beyond one day.
- Commands and review rituals can help spot graduation candidates faster.
- Graduation works best when combined with a clear human-vs-agent write boundary.

## Applications
Use this for idea pipelines, weekly reviews, project learning capture, and any workflow where daily writing should compound into durable knowledge instead of staying buried in chronology.
